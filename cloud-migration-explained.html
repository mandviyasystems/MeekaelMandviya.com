Cloud Migration

Cloud migration is the process of moving your website, data, and systems to a more reliable and scalable environment. When done properly, it improves performance, reduces costs, and makes your systems easier to manage.

At Mandviya Systems, migrations are handled strategically not every system will be migrated the same way. The right approach depends on your setup, goals, and technical requirements.

The 7 Rs Framework

We use the industry-standard 7 Rs framework to determine the best migration path for your systems.

Rehost

Move systems to the cloud with minimal changes.

Replatform

Make small improvements during migration to improve performance or reliability.

Repurchase

Replace existing systems with modern cloud-based solutions.

Refactor

Redesign systems to fully take advantage of cloud capabilities.

Retain

Keep certain systems where they are if moving them does not make sense.

Retire

Remove systems that are no longer needed or useful.

Relocate

Move entire environments without major architectural changes.

How the migration process works

1. Assessment

We review your current systems, workloads, and requirements.

2. Strategy

We determine the best migration approach using the 7 Rs framework.

3. Migration

Your systems are moved using secure, platform-based tools.

4. Testing & Stabilization

We verify everything works correctly and resolve any issues.

Why migrate?

Lower Costs: Reduce infrastructure and maintenance overhead.

Better Performance: Faster load times and improved reliability.

Scalability: Easily grow your systems as your business grows.

Security: Access to modern security standards and updates.

Accessibility: Access your systems from anywhere.
