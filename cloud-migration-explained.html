The 7 Rs Framework

We use the industry-standard 7 Rs framework to determine the best migration path for your systems.

Rehost

Move systems as-is with minimal changes.

Replatform

Make small improvements during migration.

Repurchase

Replace systems with modern cloud-based tools.

Refactor

Redesign systems to fully utilize cloud capabilities.

Retain

Keep certain systems where they are if needed.

Retire

Remove systems that are no longer useful.

Relocate

Move entire environments without redesigning them.
