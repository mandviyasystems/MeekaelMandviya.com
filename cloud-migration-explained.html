Cloud Migration

Cloud migration is the process of moving your website, data, and systems to a more reliable and scalable environment. Done correctly, it improves performance, reduces costs, and simplifies how your business operates.

At Mandviya Systems, migrations are approached strategically — not every system should be moved the same way.

The 7 Rs Framework

We use the industry-standard 7 Rs framework to determine the best migration path for your systems.

Rehost: Move systems as-is with minimal changes.

Replatform: Make small improvements during migration.

Repurchase: Replace systems with modern cloud-based tools.

Refactor: Redesign systems to fully utilize cloud capabilities.

Retain: Keep certain systems where they are if needed.

Retire: Remove systems that are no longer useful.

Relocate: Move entire environments without redesigning them.

How the migration process works

1. Assessment

We review your current setup, systems, and requirements.

2. Strategy

We determine the best approach using the 7 Rs framework.

3. Migration

Your systems are moved using secure, platform-based tools.

4. Testing & Stabilization

We verify everything works correctly and resolve issues.

Why migrate?

Lower Costs: Reduce infrastructure and maintenance overhead.

Better Performance: Faster load times and improved reliability.

Scalability: Easily grow your systems as your business grows.

Security: Access to modern security standards and updates.

Accessibility: Access your systems from anywhere.
