Pricing

Our pricing is designed to be flexible and tailored to your business needs. Below are general ranges to give you an idea of what to expect.

Basic Migration

$100 – $500

Ideal for smaller projects such as simple website migrations and low-complexity systems.

Advanced Migration

$1000 – $5000+

Designed for larger systems, workflow integrations, and full-scale cloud migrations.

Hourly Consulting

$30 – $60/hr

Best for ongoing support, troubleshooting, or smaller tasks.

Final pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and specific business requirements.
