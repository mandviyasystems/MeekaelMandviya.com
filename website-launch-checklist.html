Website Launch Checklist

Before launching a website, every business should make sure the essentials are working properly.

Domain & Hosting

Make sure your domain is connected, hosting is configured, and SSL/HTTPS is active.

Mobile Responsiveness

Test the website on desktop, tablet, and mobile to make sure it looks clean everywhere.

Contact Forms

Submit a test message to confirm forms, emails, and file uploads work correctly.

Speed & Performance

Compress images, remove unnecessary files, and make sure pages load quickly.

Final Review

Check spelling, links, buttons, navigation, and important calls-to-action before going live.
